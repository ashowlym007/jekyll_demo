和兔子小姐约好，要一起给小姑娘写信，甚至将来的某一天，会一起去南美看望她。 谁敢说这一切不会真的发生呢？正如兔子小姐的出现完全出乎我的意料一般，也许有一天， 我们之间的结局也会出乎她的意料。

给小姑娘写信还挺难的，不比向那些我爱慕过的姑娘写信容易。因为同样的未知， 同样的小心翼翼。我字斟句酌写完后，才发现这对于一个六七岁的小姑娘来说可能还是过于复杂, 于是只誊写了删节版的。下面附上未删节版。兔子小姐说好会帮我校正语法句法， 我这多年只说不练的英文水平，看样子要苦了她了。

Dear Little Girl:

Hi, we don't even know your name. What we only know is you are a little girl from South America. We must say that we are so happy to be among ones watching you to grow up. May I just call you little girl right now?

We came from China, while we are apart now. I was called Mr. Giraffe, because I have long neck. And because of shinny lovely eyes, I'd like to call her Miss. Rabbit. We used to date several times, and shared very happy memories. We are not sure where will the future go right now, so let's just wait. Little girl, would you like us to be your friends?

I, Mr. Giraffe, is a computer engineer. I work in Beijing, capital of China. It's a huge city, and the air seems always grey because of pollution, living here is not an easy thing. And my job is not easy either. However, I do enjoy my life. Because I always have hope. I have a dream, that one day after I earn enough money, and I will quit my job, go to an island and host a hotel selling beer and seafood. Every morning, I swim. Every afternoon, I have sunbath. Every night, we sing and drink. Whenever I thought about this dream, I will feel full of energy. You know, people is undefeated when they have a dream. So, do you have a dream? Do you mind share it with us?

Maybe some day in the future, Mr. Giraffe and Miss. Rabbit will go to visit you. Looking forward to your replay.

Best wishes.
